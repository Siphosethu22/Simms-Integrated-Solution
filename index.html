About Us
Welcome to Simms Integrated Solutions

Simms Integrated Solutions.cc is a proudly Namibian Small Enterprise with a rich engineering heritage and was established in 2015. With our headquarters in the coastal town of Walvis Bay we provide services to marine, mining and General Industrial sectors in Namibia. With our experience and excellent craftsmanship in multi-skilled engineering and services, we have provided our clients with the highest standards in quality, safety and reliability. Currently we specialize in the following:

  1. Marine(Ship Repairs)
  2. Structural Steelwork, Fabrication & Erection
  3. Turnkey Construction Projects
  4. Supply of Plant and Equipment for Mining & Industry
  5. Buckets Repairs & Construction
  6. Pipework, Heat Exchangers & Intercoolers
  7. Draughting & Designing
  8. Machining, Mechanical Supplies, Handling & Installation
  9. Project Management

Offered Services

Our Welding Department employs a number of highly skilled welders that are coded to internationally approved welding procedures. These welding procedures are approved to standards of societies such as the American Society of Mechanical Engineers(ASME), American Welding Society(AWS), South African National Standards(SANS) and European Welding Standards(DIN EN) and apply to a wide range of position, materials and weldings.

Our Mechanical Services Department consists of qualified and experienced artisans led by supervisors with years of experience in various industries. These personnel have contributed to the successful execution of a wide range of Mechanical maintenance operations. These include stripping down the following of inspection, as well as offering routine and prevantative maintenance services on items such as Pumps, Valves, Gearboxes, Couplings, Screens, Mills, Scrubbers Crushers Cyclones etc.

Our Electrical Services Department consists of qualified and experienced artisans led by supervisor with years of experience in various industries. These artisans have execution of a wide range of Electrical Installation, maintenance of electrical motors, welding machines and lighting system. Other tasks executed are stripping down the following for inspection, as well as offering routine and preventative maintenance services on items such as: Electrical Motor de ac, Welding Machines, Generator, Alternators, etc

Our Fabrication Department has qualified steel workers who are capable of fabricating a large range of itmes from ferrous to non-ferrous materials. This includes steel structure, bins, chutes, hoppers,low pressure and high pressure vessels, piping, conveyors, water tight doors, platforms, hatch cover, tanks, cell guides, gangways, repair of earth moving equipment(buckets) and modular plant components.
